About Me

I am a staff research scientist at InnoPeak Technology (a.k.a., OPPO US Research Center), focusing on XR related projects. From 2017 to 2021, I served as a senior research scientist at JD.com American Technologies in Mountain View, CA. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Wuhan University, 2017.

My interests primarily focus on the field of 3D Computer Vision and Graphics. In particular, I tackle the challenges in scene reconstruction and understanding, including neural surface reconstruction, SLAM, RGB-D fusion, depth estimation, semantic segmentation and view synthesis.
