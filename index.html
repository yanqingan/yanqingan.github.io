About Me

I am a computer vision scientist at JD.com Silicon Valley Research Center in Mountain View, CA. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Wuhan University, 2017. In JD, I work on fashion-related applications, mainly involved in virtual human shaping and clothing fitting.

My interests primarily focus on the field of 3D computer vision and graphics. In particular, I tackle the challenges in structure from motion, RGB-D fusion, depth estimation, image synthesis and visual correspondence. I am also interested in 3D deep learning, such as 3D scene perception, guided shape recovery and unstructured data representation.
