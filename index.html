About Me

I am a computer vision scientist at JD.com Silicon Valley Research Center in Mountain View, CA. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Wuhan University, 2017.

My work focus on the field of computer vision and graphics. In particular, I tackle the challenges in structure from motion, multi-view stereo, SLAM, 2D&3D correspondence and fusion. I am also interested in geometric deep learning, such as 3D scene understanding, guided shape recovery and unstructured data organizing.
