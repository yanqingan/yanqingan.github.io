About Me

I am a research scientist at JD.com Silicon Valley Research Center in Mountain View, CA. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Wuhan University, 2017. In JD, I work on 3D related applications, mainly involved in virtual clothing fitting and efficient object reconstruction.

My interests primarily focus on the field of Computer Vision and Graphics. In particular, I tackle the challenges in structure from motion, multi-view stereo, RGB-D fusion, depth estimation, image synthesis and visual correspondence. I am also interested in data-driven 3D object analysis, such as geometric scene perception, template-based shape recovery and unstructured data representation.
