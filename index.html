About Me

I am a computer vision scientist at JD.com Silicon Valley Research Center in Mountain View, CA. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Wuhan University, in 2017, under the supervision of Prof.Chunxia Xiao.

My work focus on the field of computer vision and graphics. In particular, I tackle the challenges in structure from motion, multi-view stereo, SLAM, image dense correspondence and fusion. I am also interested in geometric deep learning, such as 3D scene understanding, guided shape recovery and unstructured data organizing.
