Curriculum Vitae 

Short Bio

I am a computer vision scientist at JD.com Silicon Valley Research Center in Santa Clara, California. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Wuhan University, China, in 2017, under the supervision of Prof.Chunxia Xiao.

My work focus on the field of computer vision and AR/VR. In particular, I tackle the challenges in 3D reconstruction, SLAM, image and RGBD data analysis. I am also interested in the application of deep learning within 3D field, such as semantic shape understanding and scene recovery.

If you are interested in collaboration with me, please feel free to send me an email at yanqinganssg@gmail.com.
